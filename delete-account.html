Deleting your Heap App account

1. Open Heap App

Locate the app on your phone and open it.

2. Tap the profile icon

Navigate to home and tap your user icon on the top left of the page.

3. Tap Delete account

Tap the Delete account button. When prompted to confirm the deletion of your account, tap yes.

If you don't have access to the app

Please email us at hello@getheap.app with a request to delete your account if you are unable to access the mobile app.
